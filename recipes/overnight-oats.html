Overnight Oats Recipe

Description

Overnight oats is a great breakfast or snacks. Its a no-cook
method of making oatmeal, ready-to-eat after resting in the fridge
overnight.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Chia seeds
  • Greek or vegan yogurt
  • Vanilla extract
  • Sweetener
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Combine old fashioned oats and chia seeds by using a bowl,
    a mason jar, or just some glass cups

  2. Add the Greek or vegan yogurt and vanilla extract.

  3. Add the sweetener of choice and the milk. Then mix the
    overnight oats together until you see no clumps. Seal or cover
    with a lid, and chill in the fridge for at least 2 hours or overnight.

  4. In the next morning, grab a spoon, add your favorite toppings
    and dig in.
